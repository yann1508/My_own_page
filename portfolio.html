Project 1

{Tek}Bot

TekBot is an association of robotics and electronics made up of students in 1st and 2nd year of computer science at Epitech. During the year we had to realize a challenge which consists in realizing an intelligent car with materials put at our disposal.
As material we had
-a power source including a flat battery
-the arduino card which is a microcontroller
-the drive or the wheels of the car
-the servo-motor which is a particular motor that allows to turn the sensor
-the ultrasonic sensor which is like the eyes of the car and allows to measure the distance to an obstacle.
My team and I had two weeks to research and understand the function of the different components of the car and how to mount them. After that we went to the code part on the Arduino IDE and uploaded it to the arduino board.

Project 2

HealthTech Challenge

The Healthtech challenge is a challenge organized by Semé city in partnership with the Ministry of Health and the Belgian Development Agency Enabel. The goal of this challenge was to propose technological innovations to improve health services in Benin. For this fact 03 themes were proposed to us. The first theme concerns the reminder of prenatal consultation and family planning appointments, the second on the real-time verification of the availability of blood products in blood banks for health professionals and the encouragement of the public to participate in the organization or blood drives and the third and last theme on the management of the fleet and movements of ambulances. This challenge was carried out in 2 phases. The first phase was the field phase where we had to investigate in different cities namely: Cotonou, Abomey-Calavi, Porto-Novo, Allada or Sô Ava. We went in contact with the populations, made observations, asked questions and collected the necessary data to propose an innovative solution in connection with the chosen theme. My group and I had the third theme which was the management of the fleet and the movement of ambulances. The second phase was the idea phase where we had to present facts in front of a jury. It was a great experience even though my team and I did not make it to the second round of the second phase.
